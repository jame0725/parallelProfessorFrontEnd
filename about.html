Our Mission

Over 40% of college students struggle to keep up with coursework due to unclear lectures or limited access to professors, while one in four regularly misses class. Parallel Professor bridges this gap by allowing professors to record lectures, which AI then summarizes and pairs with a chatbot for 24/7, context-aware answers. This solution helps students catch up, learn at their own pace, and review material asynchronously, all while reducing the burden on professors. In a digital age, Parallel Professor makes education more accessible and personalized, giving every student a better chance to succeed.
